Craftsmanship with Heart

Redemption Renovations began as a way to provide for our family and grew into a mission to treat every home like our own. We believe in building the kind of relationships that outlast the projects themselves.

Our Story

We started in a small workshop with a borrowed table saw and a big desire to do things the right way. Word spread quickly because people felt heard, respected, and taken care of. Today, we lead a full team of builders, designers, and craftsmen who share those same values.

"The best part of our week is still sitting down with homeowners at their kitchen table, napkin sketches in hand, and dreaming about what comes next."

What Guides Us

Integrity

No surprises, no cut corners. We communicate clearly and own our work.

Craft

Details matter. From rough framing to trim, we approach every phase like it will carry our name.

Community

We show up for local events, support neighbors, and volunteer our skills whenever we can.

Clarity

Renovations are big commitments. We keep the process transparent so you always know the next step.
