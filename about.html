About Us

Craftsmanship with Heart

Redemption Renovations began as a way to provide for our family and grew into a mission to treat every home like our own. We believe in building the kind of relationships that outlast the projects themselves.

Our Journey

From Workshop to Your Home

We started in a small workshop with a borrowed table saw and a big desire to do things the right way. Word spread quickly because people felt heard, respected, and taken care of.

Today, we lead a full team of builders, designers, and craftsmen who share those same values. The best part of our week is still sitting down with homeowners at their kitchen table, napkin sketches in hand, dreaming about what comes next.
